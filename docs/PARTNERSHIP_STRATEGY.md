**Awareness:**
- Impressions from co-marketing
- Brand mentions
- Traffic from partner

**Acquisition:**
- Signups attributed to partner
- Wallet connections
- First trades

**Activation:**
- Conversion rates from partner traffic
- Quality of users (LTV)

**Economic:**
- Cost of partnership (money, time, resources)
- Revenue generated
- ROI

## Best Practices

### DO:
- Start with small, low-risk partnerships
- Always track attribution (UTM codes, referral links)
- Be transparent about goals and expectations
- Deliver on commitments (build reputation)
- Nurture relationships (not transactional)
- Think long-term (partnerships compound)

### DON'T:
- Over-promise and under-deliver
- Hide important information
- Demand exclusivity unless you're paying for it
- Ignore partners after deal closes
- Burn bridges (crypto is small)
- Partner with scammy projects (kills your credibility)
